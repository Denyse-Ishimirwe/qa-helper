

QA Helper — Month 2

Day by Day Plan

April 2026


The goal: A real QA tester, on a real Irembo form, gets accurate results in under 2 minutes. Manual testing takes 1 hour. The tool does the same thing in 60 seconds. That is the demo that matters.


How to use this plan with Cursor: Each day has a &quot;What To Tell Cursor&quot; column. Copy that text and paste it into Cursor as your prompt. Don’t worry about the code — Cursor writes it. Your job is to understand what each step does and confirm it works before moving to the next day.
